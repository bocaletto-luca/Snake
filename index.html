Official Snake Rules

Objective:
Control the snake to eat food and grow. The snake moves on a grid with a wrap-around effect: it exits on one side and appears on the opposite side. At advanced levels (up to 20), fixed obstacles (blocks) are introduced which will end the game if touched.

Controls:
Use the arrow keys or WASD, click or drag on the canvas, or use a gamepad to change direction.

Levels:
Each level requires you to eat a certain amount of food to progress to the next level; as the level increases, the speed increases and obstacles are introduced.

Good luck and have fun!
